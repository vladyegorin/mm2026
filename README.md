# The Good, The Bad, and The... That’s It

A chaotic, Undertale-inspired coding experience where two forces spectate your LeetCode journey:

- 😇 **Cleany** - the calm advocate for understanding, clean code, and growth.
- 😈 **Vibey** - the speed-obsessed AI demon who just wants to solve everything for you.

Both want you to succeed.

Just… in very different ways.

---

## 🎮 Concept

This project simulates a coding interview environment with live commentary from two characters:

- Solve problems yourself and build intuition.
- Let AI take over and experience the consequences.
- Or try to balance both.

Through escalating difficulty (Easy → Medium → Hard), the player experiences:

- The temptation of instant AI solutions  
- The discomfort of struggle  
- The satisfaction of understanding  

It’s not about rejecting AI.

It’s about **using it intentionally**.

---

## 🧠 Core Message

You can’t escape AI.

But you can control how you use it.

Understanding what you write matters — especially when it counts.

Stay determined.
